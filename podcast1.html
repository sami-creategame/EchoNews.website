Hosted by MERCIER Alexis, ODIN Baptiste, GAIN-BROCHET Elliot & JABER Sami January 2026

This podcast explores the major transformations shaping society in 2025.

It discusses digital isolation and the rise of digital minimalism in response to constant connectivity.

It also analyzes the evolution of news and information, between influencers and traditional journalists.

The podcast examines the impact of automation and artificial intelligence on the future of work.

Overall, it offers insights on how to adapt to technological change while staying human.
